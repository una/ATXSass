Missed SassConf in New York and CSSDevConf in New Orleans? Don’t worry! This month’s meetup focuses on what’s new in Sass this year. We’re hosting a panel of attendees and speakers, and will have a recap and resources to get your wheels turning!

The ATX Sass Meetup is run by Ian Carrico, Una Kravets, and Elyse Holladay.
